AiGuessr?

About AiGuessr

Welcome to AI Mashup Guesser!

The goal of the game is to guess which two characters/people/things have been mashed together. So, for example if you think the image above is a result of mashing together Picard and Abraham Lincoln -- you would put Picard in one of the answer boxes, and Abraham Lincoln in the other.

  • The answers are not case sensitive, but do need to match exactly otherwise.
  • If you feel you got it right but it won't accept it, there's no shame in using the "give up" or "reveal random keyword" buttons to help you.
  • Most answers are proper nouns, either real people or fictional characters.
  • Some are "things" but these are rare.

This pack has been crafted specifically for submission to Vinesauce, as such some of the answers are specifically tailored to the stream's culture/memes.

More mashups may be added in the future! To see this help again, click on the near the top of the page.
